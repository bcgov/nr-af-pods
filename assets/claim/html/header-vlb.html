The Veterinary and Veterinary Technologists Locums provides funding for veterinarians and veterinary technologists to provide locum services to veterinary practices that offer food animal services in BC.

Important Information!

Before proceeding with your application, please ensure the following:

  • You have reviewed the program information Agriculture and seafood programs - Province of British Columbia (gov.bc.ca)
  • You are prepared to upload proof of your professional license (CVBC or BCVTA)
  • You know your CRA business number (if applicable)
