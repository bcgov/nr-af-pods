B.C. Agri-Business Planning Program - Claim for Payment

The B.C. Agri-Business Planning Program (ABPP) provides funding support for primary agricultural producers and food processors to support informed decision making and ensure business development.

Stream 1: Agri-Business and Leadership Development

Instructions:
  • Complete all sections of claim for payment form.
  • Attach copy of the Invoice(s) from Learning Provider(s) (as per approved learning action plan)
  • Complete Program Evaluation Survey (your feedback is important to us to inform program development to better support producers such as yourselves).

The Ministry may request further information if required for confirmation purposes.
